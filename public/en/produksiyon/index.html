01 — Visual LanguageFive Principles

We distilled the brief's essence into five words. Every frame must carry at least three of them — otherwise it doesn't make the cut.

01

Minimal composition

The subject must read cleanly in every frame. No complex props, crowded framing, or unnecessary accessories.

02

Natural light

80% of shots in golden hour and blue hour. Studio lighting only as interior fill.

03

Lens flare ↦ friend

No anti-reflective filters. Natural flare is part of the composition.

04

Cast = atmosphere

1–3 figures. Spontaneous and genuine. Faces are welcome, sales poses are not — moments, not poses.

05

Warm neutral

A warm half-stop. Not cliché — character. The brand's signature palette always leans warm.

Depth & Contrast

In brand use: all headlines with negative tracking (-1%), no all-caps. For CTAs and menus, all-caps Inter Medium 11px, letter-spacing +320‰.
